Specialized Services

We are much more than just a hotel chain! With locations near a temple, we offer many services to temple patrons!

Free and low-cost services for temple patrons

  • Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for patrons who have traveled long distance
  • A playroom and low-cost, short-term childcare
  • Temple history themed reception and sitting area
  • A family search center that supports family search and ordinance work
  • Access to our beautiful grounds and covered picnic area

Wedding reception services

Choose from 3 reception halls and an outdoor area to customize your perfect day! With a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving, if you prefer to do it yourself!
