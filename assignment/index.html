Your temple experience starts here

We here at Temple Inn & Suites have beautiful accomodations to meet a wide variety of needs. Whether you are a new bride needing rooms for family members or a place to have your reception, or are traveling to attend the temple, we are sure to help make your temple experience a haven of beauty and peace.

Located near the temple

We are a hotel chain with many locations, each near a Latter-day Saint temple. Our standards are high as you would expect with a company who's main customer base are temple patrons! Some of the services we offer are room and suite reservations for long and short term rates, reception hall rental, catering, babysitting and free changing rooms available to all long distance temple patrons.
