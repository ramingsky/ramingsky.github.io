Family Free Fishing Day

By Rasmus Jilligan

It's that time of year again as residents of Fish Haven gear up for the annual local Family Free Fishing Day event to be held on April 22 from 7am until 11am. This will be held at Potter's park. Come early to see Mayor Green make the first cast! Free doughnuts and milk will be served. Loads of prizes for both children and adults in all age ranges. Fish and Wildlife will be on hand to give out information and licensing information for the season.

If you are interested in donating to this tax deductable cause, or to volunteer for the event, please contact Frank Smiley at 333-3333.
